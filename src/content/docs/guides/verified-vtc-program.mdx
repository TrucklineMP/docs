# Verified VTC Program Rules

The Verified VTC Program recognizes authentic, active, and well-managed Virtual Trucking Companies on TrucklineMP. Verified VTCs receive a blue verification badge and access to additional platform features.

## How to Apply

### Eligibility

- You must be the **owner** of the VTC or hold the `management.vtc.edit` permission.
- Your VTC must **not** already be verified.
- The Verified VTC Program must be **currently accepting applications** (check the status on your VTC's Verified Status page).

### Application Process

1. Navigate to your VTC's management panel.
2. Select **Verified Status** from the sidebar.
3. Review the program requirements displayed on the page.
4. Click **Apply for Verification** to open the application form.
5. Complete all required questions accurately.
6. (Optional) Provide a Discord webhook URL to receive status notifications.
7. Submit your application.

### After Submission

- Your application will enter **Pending Review** status.
- The Truckline moderation team will review your submission.
- You may be moved to **Under Review** while your application is being evaluated.
- You will be notified of the decision via:
  - The status displayed on your VTC's Verified Status page.
  - A Discord webhook notification (if provided).

### Reapplication

- If your application is **rejected**, you must wait **30 days** before reapplying.
- If your application is **withdrawn**, you may submit a new application immediately.

### Withdrawing an Application

- You may withdraw your application at any time while it is **Pending** or **Under Review**.
- Withdrawn applications can be replaced with a new submission immediately.

## Requirements

The following requirements must be met for your VTC to be approved for verification:

### General Requirements

- Your VTC must be a **legitimate and active** virtual trucking community.
- Your VTC must have a **complete profile**, including:
  - A valid VTC name and handle.
  - A description or slogan.
  - Contact information (website, email, Discord, or social media).
- Your VTC must comply with all [TrucklineMP Terms of Service]({LEGAL_URLS.termsOfService}) and community guidelines.

### Cross-Platform Membership

If your VTC maintains an active profile on another virtual trucking platform (for example, TruckersMP), a portion of the drivers on that **external** roster must also be members of your VTC on TrucklineMP. The required overlap scales with your external roster size — larger communities are expected to have a stronger presence here.

This requirement applies when you have one or more external profiles linked or disclosed in your application (e.g. a TruckersMP VTC page URL). If you have **no** external VTC profile, this section does not apply.

#### Overlap tiers

Use the row that matches the **driver count on your largest disclosed external VTC profile**. You must meet **both** the percentage and the minimum absolute count for that tier (whichever calculation requires more members wins).

| External drivers | Required overlap on TrucklineMP           |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| **1–19**         | At least **8** members                    |
| **20–49**        | At least **25%**, minimum **12** members  |
| **50–99**        | At least **30%**, minimum **18** members  |
| **100–199**      | At least **35%**, minimum **40** members  |
| **200–399**      | At least **40%**, minimum **90** members  |
| **400+**         | At least **45%**, minimum **160** members |

**Examples**

- 15 drivers on TruckersMP → need **8** on TrucklineMP.
- 40 drivers on TruckersMP → 25% = 10, but the tier minimum is **12** → need **12** on TrucklineMP.
- 80 drivers on TruckersMP → 30% = **24** on TrucklineMP.
- 150 drivers on TruckersMP → 35% = **53** on TrucklineMP.
- 350 drivers on TruckersMP → 40% = **140** on TrucklineMP.

Percentages are rounded **up** to the nearest whole driver. Staff count overlap from the external roster you disclose — inactive or duplicate entries may be excluded at review.

#### At application

- You must meet the tier for your external roster size **at the time of application**.
- Applications below the required overlap may be rejected until TrucklineMP membership catches up.

#### After verification

- You are expected to stay within your tier. A sustained drop **more than 10 percentage points below** your tier's percentage, or below your tier's absolute minimum, may trigger a review and can affect verified status.
- Short-term dips (e.g. after a recruitment drive on another platform) are understood; contact staff if you expect a temporary change.

### Application Questions

The application form includes questions configured by the Truckline team. These may include:

- Your VTC's TruckersMP page URL (if applicable).
- Details about your VTC's structure, rules, and activity.
- Links to your VTC's Discord server or website.
- A Discord webhook URL for receiving verification status notifications.

**All required fields must be completed truthfully.** Incomplete or misleading applications will be rejected.

## Benefits of Verified Status

Verified VTCs receive the following benefits:

| Benefit                 | Standard VTC | Verified VTC             |
| ----------------------- | ------------ | ------------------------ |
| Verification Badge      | No           | Yes (blue checkmark)     |
| Custom Roles            | Up to 20     | **Unlimited**            |
| Directory Visibility    | Standard     | Highlighted with badge   |
| Profile Badge           | None         | Blue "Verified" flair    |
| Custom Page Layout      | No           | **Yes**                  |
| Community Trust         | Standard     | Enhanced credibility     |
| Beta Tester Recruitment | Standard     | **Priority recruitment** |

### Verification Badge

- A blue checkmark badge is displayed next to your VTC's name throughout the platform.
- The badge appears on your VTC profile, in the VTC directory, and in global news feeds.

### Unlimited Custom Roles

- Verified VTCs can create an **unlimited number of custom roles** for their organizational hierarchy.
- Standard VTCs are limited to 20 roles.

### Enhanced Visibility

- Verified VTCs are highlighted in the VTC directory with a blue "Verified" badge.
- Your VTC profile displays a prominent "Verified" flair with the description: _"This VTC has been verified for authenticity."_

### Priority Beta Tester Recruitment

- All members of Verified VTCs receive **priority status** when applying to become TrucklineMP beta testers.
- This priority applies to every member of your VTC, not just leadership.
- Members must still complete the beta NDA verification process.
- Priority status is automatically applied when a member accepts the beta NDA using their linked Discord account.

### Custom Page Layout and Sections

- Verified VTCs can **rearrange their VTC page website content** using the Home Layout builder.
- You can **add custom profile sections** to showcase your community, rules, recruitment, or other information.
- Manage this from your VTC management panel under **Appearance** → **Profile Sections** and **Home Layout** (requires the `management.vtc.appearance` permission).

## Rules for Maintaining Verified Status

Once your VTC is verified, you must continue to comply with the following rules to maintain your status.

### Compliance Requirements

1. **Adhere to all TrucklineMP rules and policies.** Any violations may result in immediate removal of verified status.
2. **Maintain an accurate and up-to-date VTC profile.** This includes:
   - A valid and active contact method (website, Discord, or email).
   - Accurate VTC information and description.
3. **Do not engage in fraudulent or deceptive practices.** This includes:
   - Falsifying membership numbers.
   - Impersonating other VTCs or organizations.
   - Misrepresenting your VTC's activities or affiliations.
4. **Maintain active VTC operations.** Verified VTCs that become inactive or abandoned may have their status revoked.
5. **Do not use AI-generated media.** Verified VTCs may not use AI-generated content as part of their official VTC presence on TrucklineMP (see below).

### No AI-Generated Media

Verified VTCs (and Partnered VTCs) **may not** use AI-generated content for their public VTC presence on TrucklineMP. This prohibition includes, but is not limited to:

- AI-generated profile pictures
- AI-generated banners
- AI-generated website content or landing pages presented as your VTC's official site
- Any other AI-generated images, video, or media used in your VTC profile, page sections, or branding

Using AI-generated media may result in removal of verified or partnered status and a requirement to replace affected assets.

### Prohibited Actions

The following actions may result in immediate removal of verified status:

- Violating the TrucklineMP Terms of Service.
- Engaging in harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory behavior.
- Using the VTC for illegal activities.
- Falsifying information on your VTC profile or application.
- Transferring VTC ownership to an ineligible user.
- Failing to respond to moderation inquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
- Using AI-generated profile pictures, banners, website content, or other AI-generated media on your VTC page or as official VTC branding.

### Status Revocation

- Verified status may be revoked by the Truckline moderation team at any time if your VTC fails to comply with these rules.
- If your verified status is revoked, you may reapply after addressing the issues that led to the revocation.
- Revocation is permanent for the current application cycle. You must submit a new application after the revocation.

### Name Change Restrictions

- Verified VTCs are subject to name change cooldowns to prevent abuse.
- Frequent or suspicious name changes may trigger a review of your verified status.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does the review process take?

Review times vary depending on the volume of applications. Most applications are reviewed within 7-14 days.

### Can I edit my application after submitting?

No. Once submitted, you cannot edit your application. If you need to make changes, withdraw your application and submit a new one.

### What happens if my application is rejected?

You will receive a review note explaining the reason for the rejection. You may reapply after 30 days.

### Can I appeal a rejection?

There is no formal appeal process. However, you may submit a new application after the 30-day cooldown with additional information or corrections.

### Does verified status expire?

Verified status does not have a fixed expiration date. However, it may be revoked if your VTC fails to comply with the program rules or becomes inactive.

### Can I lose my verified status?

Yes. Verified status may be revoked for violations of TrucklineMP rules, fraudulent activity, or prolonged inactivity.

### Is there a cost for verified status?

No. The Verified VTC Program is free for all eligible VTCs.

### Can verified VTC owners get a role on the main Discord?

Yes. After your VTC is verified, you can request the **Verified VTC Manager/Owner** role on the official TrucklineMP Discord. See [Verified VTC Discord Role](/guides/verified-vtc-discord-role/).
