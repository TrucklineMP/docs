# Regional and Timezone

TrucklineMP uses your timezone preference to show local dates and times on events, notifications, and other time-sensitive content.

## Accessing regional settings

1. Click your avatar in the top-right corner
2. Select **Settings**
3. Open the **Regional** tab
4. Or go to `trucklinemp.com/user/me/settings?tab=regional`

---

## Choosing a timezone

Select the IANA timezone that matches where you live or where you plan convoys. Examples:

- `Europe/London`
- `America/New_York`
- `Europe/Berlin`

Changes save when you confirm. Relative times (for example "2 hours ago") update on the next page load.

---

## What uses your timezone

| Feature | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Events** | Start and end times shown in your local offset |
| **Notifications** | Timestamps in the bell popover |
| **Profile activity** | Localized display where supported |
| **Forum** | Datetime rendering when forum uses your account preference |

Some VTC tools (such as announcement **quiet hours**) use a timezone configured on the webhook, not your personal setting. See [VTC Announcements](/guides/vtc-announcements/).

---

## Best practices

- Pick the timezone where you actually schedule events, not necessarily your physical location if you lead convoys in another region
- Update your timezone when you move or change your primary play schedule
- Event creators should still enter accurate UTC-backed times in the event editor; viewers see converted local times

---

## Troubleshooting

### Times look wrong by one hour

Daylight saving changes are handled by IANA zones. Re-select your timezone if you recently traveled.

### Event still shows a different time than Discord

Discord messages and TrucklineMP event pages may use different display rules. Trust the event page on trucklinemp.com for official RSVP times.
