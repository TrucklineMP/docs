# Forum

The TrucklineMP forum is a separate community site for discussions, guides, and thread-based conversation. It shares your TrucklineMP account.

## Forum URL

Visit [forum.trucklinemp.com](https://forum.trucklinemp.com).

Sign in with the same account you use on the main site. Your forum identity uses your TrucklineMP profile name, handle, and avatar.

---

## Account link

No extra registration is required when SSO is enabled. If prompted to connect:

1. Sign in on trucklinemp.com first
2. Return to the forum and choose **Sign in with TrucklineMP** (or equivalent)
3. Approve the session

Your forum posts appear on your [public profile](/guides/public-profile/) **Forum** tab when that module is active.

---

## Forum settings

Configure preferences on the main site:

1. Open **Settings**
2. Go to the **Forum** tab (`/user/me/settings?tab=forum`)

Options typically include:

- Notification preferences for replies and mentions
- Display preferences for how threads render
- Signature text appended to your posts

Broader theme settings live under [Appearance & Preferences](/guides/appearance-preferences/).

---

## Notifications

Forum notifications may appear in:

- The forum's own notification UI
- Your TrucklineMP [notification bell](/guides/notifications/) when cross-posted
- Email (if you enable email notifications on the forum)

Check both sites if you expect a reply.

---

## Moderation

Forum moderation follows TrucklineMP [rules](https://id.trucklinemp.com/legal/rules) and forum-specific policies. Severe violations may affect your main platform standing, not just forum access.

Report posts using the forum's report tools. Platform-wide appeals for bans still go through [Support](/guides/support-tickets/).

---

## Data export

Your [data export](/guides/appearance-preferences/#data-export) on the main site can include forum posts and threads associated with your account.
