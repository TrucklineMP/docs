# Support Tickets

The support system lets you contact TrucklineMP staff through structured tickets. Track replies, attach context, and keep a record of your request.

## Accessing support

Open [trucklinemp.com/support](https://trucklinemp.com/support) while signed in.

From the support hub you can:

- View your open and closed tickets
- Start a new ticket by category (lane)
- Jump to ban appeals (see [Bans & Appeals](/guides/bans-appeals/))

---

## Support lanes

Tickets are grouped by **lane** so the right team handles your request:

| Lane | Typical use |
|------|-------------|
| **General** | Account help, bugs, questions |
| **Player report** | Report another user's behavior |
| **Appeals** | Challenge a ban or suspension |
| **Other specialized lanes** | Shown on the support hub when available |

Each lane has its own create form with fields matched to the issue type.

---

## Creating a ticket

1. Go to **Support**
2. Pick the lane that fits your issue
3. Click **New ticket** (or the lane card)
4. Fill in the form (title, description, category-specific fields)
5. Submit

You receive a ticket ID and can return to `/support/{id}` for the conversation thread.

---

## Player reports

From another user's profile, use **Report user** to open a pre-filled player report ticket. Include accurate details. False reports may be actioned against the reporter.

---

## Ticket conversation

On the ticket page you can:

- Read staff replies
- Add follow-up messages while the ticket is open
- See status (open, awaiting reply, closed)
- View linked moderation context for appeals

Keep one topic per ticket. Open a new ticket for unrelated issues.

---

## Appeals shortcut

Ban appeals use the appeals lane. You can start from:

- [trucklinemp.com/support?lane=appeal](https://trucklinemp.com/support?lane=appeal)
- The appeals card on the support hub
- Links from [Bans & Appeals](/guides/bans-appeals/)

## Category cheat sheet: what to do and what not

No fake professionalism here. Straight, honest tickets get resolved faster than polished ones. A quick do and don't for each category:

### General help

- Do: say exactly what you were trying to do and what actually happened
- Do: include order IDs, usernames, or timestamps if the issue involves them
- Don't: open a ticket before checking [the status page](https://status.trucklinemp.com) if something feels like it is down for everyone
- Don't: bundle three unrelated questions into one ticket

### Player report

- Do: attach a screenshot or clip. Without evidence this almost always gets closed
- Do: say what rule you think was broken, not just "this guy was bad"
- Don't: report the same incident more than once, or get friends to pile on with duplicate reports
- Don't: report someone for something that annoyed you but was not actually against the rules, check [the rules](https://id.trucklinemp.com/legal/rules) first

### VTC report

- Do: try resolving member or role disputes with the VTC owner first, most of those are not staff's problem
- Do: bring evidence for scams, impersonation, or ownership abuse claims
- Don't: use this to settle a personal grudge with a VTC's leadership
- Don't: expect us to referee normal VTC internal drama

### Ban appeal

- Do: answer "Was that kinda on you?" honestly, Yes, No, or Depends all get read the same way
- Do: explain why it happened in plain language, no legal-sounding phrasing needed
- Don't: fake being sorry. If you don't think it was you, say so. If it was you, say that too
- Don't: file a second appeal for the same ban while the first is still open, it just resets your place in line

### Bug report

- Do: give clear steps to reproduce, what you expected, and what actually happened
- Do: mention your browser or client version, it saves a round trip
- Don't: file five reports for the same underlying bug across different pages
- Don't: skip the repro steps and just say "it's broken," that gets deprioritized fast

### Feature request

- Do: explain the actual problem you are trying to solve, not just the feature you imagine
- Don't: expect a shipping timeline in the reply, ideas get reviewed, not promised

### Feedback

- Do: be specific about what happened and what would have felt better
- Do: use this for staff interaction feedback too, we read it
- Don't: use feedback as a backdoor player or VTC report, use the report categories for rule breaks instead

## Official Discord server

Support for the **main TrucklineMP Discord** (moderation bot tickets) is separate from website support. Website tickets are always handled at trucklinemp.com. See [Official Discord Server](/guides/official-discord-server/).

---

## Best practices

- Be specific: dates, usernames, links, screenshots
- One person per account; do not have friends duplicate your ticket
- Check [rules](https://id.trucklinemp.com/legal/rules) before appealing a ban
- Allow reasonable time for staff response during peak periods
