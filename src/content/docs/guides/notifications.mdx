# Notifications

TrucklineMP sends in-app notifications for activity that concerns you: VTC updates, recruitment, events, moderation notices, and more.

## Notification bell

Signed-in users see a **bell icon** in the site header.

- A badge shows how many unread notifications you have
- Click the bell to open the popover list
- Click a notification to open the related page (when a link is available)
- Mark individual items read or clear the list from the popover

There is no separate full-page notification center yet. The bell is the primary interface.

---

## What you might receive

Examples include:

- VTC application accepted or rejected
- New recruitment application (for VTC staff)
- Event reminders or RSVP changes
- Platform announcements
- Moderation or account notices (when applicable)

Exact types depend on your roles and activity.

---

## Discord DM delivery

If your **Discord account is linked** under [Connections](/guides/connections/), the TrucklineMP Discord bot may deliver the same notification as a **direct message** on Discord.

Requirements:

- Discord connected at `trucklinemp.com/user/me/settings?tab=connections`
- Your Discord account allows DMs from server members / the TrucklineMP bot
- The notification type is eligible for Discord relay

DM delivery is a convenience copy. Always check the website bell for the authoritative list.

See [Discord Notifications](/guides/discord-notifications/) for troubleshooting DMs.

---

## Mobile app

The TrucklineMP mobile app has its own notification APIs. Mobile push is separate from the website bell but may show similar items.

---

## Privacy

Notifications are private to your account. Other users cannot see your notification list.

---

## Troubleshooting

### Bell shows a count but the list is empty

Refresh the page. If it persists, sign out and back in.

### Not receiving Discord DMs

Confirm Discord is linked, DMs are open, and you are not blocked by the bot. See [Discord Notifications](/guides/discord-notifications/).

### Missing recruitment alerts

You need the correct VTC staff permissions and recruitment must be enabled for your VTC.
