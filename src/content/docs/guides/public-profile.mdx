# Public Profile

Your public profile is how other drivers see you on TrucklineMP. It combines identity, VTC membership, community activity, and standing information.

## Opening a profile

- Your profile: click your avatar, or go to `trucklinemp.com/user/me` (redirects to your handle or WebID URL)
- Another user: `trucklinemp.com/@handle` or `/user/{webId}`
- From the [user directory](/guides/user-directory/)

---

## Profile header

The header shows:

- Avatar and display name
- Handle (`@username`)
- WebID
- Staff or program badges
- Active ban or suspension banner (when applicable)
- Live **Twitch** or **YouTube** status when connected and visible
- Quick actions (report user, for signed-in viewers)

---

## Profile tabs

Tabs vary by account. Common sections:

### Overview

Summary stats, roles, badges, and highlights.

### VTC

Current VTC membership, role, and link to the company profile. Users in no VTC show an empty state.

### Activity

Recent platform activity feed (posts, participation, and similar signals when available).

### Forum

Forum participation summary with links to [forum.trucklinemp.com](https://forum.trucklinemp.com) content when the forum module is enabled.

### Configs

External truck configs you chose to share (hidden when you enable **hide external configs** in settings).

### Record

Ban and moderation history visible under standing rules. You may see more on your own profile than strangers do. See [Bans & Appeals](/guides/bans-appeals/).

---

## What you control

Edit in [Profile Settings](/guides/profile-settings/):

- Display name and handle
- Avatar (upload or URL)
- Social links (TikTok, X, etc.)
- YouTube and Twitch visibility toggles

Edit in [Connections](/guides/connections/):

- Linked OAuth providers (Steam, Discord, Google, etc.)

---

## Badges and programs

Recognition badges (for example contributor or program awards) appear when staff or automated programs assign them. You cannot self-assign official badges.

---

## Reporting

Signed-in users can report a profile for moderation review. Reports create a [support ticket](/guides/support-tickets/). Provide factual details.

---

## API

Public profile data is available to integrations:

```
GET https://api.trucklinemp.com/users/{handleOrId}
```

OAuth `profile` scope returns a subset for authorized apps. See [OAuth Apps](/guides/developers/oauth-apps/).
