# Verified VTC Discord Role

Verified VTC owners can request a special role on the **main TrucklineMP Discord server**: **Verified VTC Manager/Owner**. This role is for VTC leadership on the official community server. It is separate from your VTC's own Discord server and from [VTC Discord Verification](/guides/vtc-discord-verification/) on your profile.

The system is fully automated. Requests are checked against your TrucklineMP account and VTC permissions. No manual staff approval is required for a valid request.

---

## What you get

- The **Verified VTC Manager/Owner** role on the main TrucklineMP Discord
- Recognition as leadership of a [Verified VTC](/guides/verified-vtc-program/)
- Up to **two** role holders per VTC:
  - The **VTC owner** (required slot)
  - **One optional manager** from your VTC

---

## Requirements

Before you request the role, make sure all of the following are true.

### For the VTC owner (you)

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Verified VTC** | Your VTC must have approved [Verified VTC Program](/guides/verified-vtc-program/) status on TrucklineMP |
| **Ownership** | You must be the VTC owner. Only the owner can submit the request |
| **Linked Discord** | Your TrucklineMP account must be linked to the Discord account you use on the main server |
| **Verified linked role** | You must have the **Verified** [Linked Role](/guides/discord-linked-roles/) on the main Discord server |
| **Main server member** | You must be a member of the official TrucklineMP Discord |

### For the optional manager

If you nominate a second person, they must also:

- Be a **member** of your VTC on TrucklineMP
- Hold a **Management** position in that VTC (the role with VTC management permissions)
- Have their TrucklineMP account **linked to Discord**
- Have the **Verified** Linked Role on the main Discord server
- Not already hold this role for another VTC

You cannot nominate yourself twice. The owner slot and manager slot are separate.

---

## How to request the role

Requests are made on the main TrucklineMP Discord in the dedicated **Verified VTC role** channel (posted by Modbot).

1. Join the [official TrucklineMP Discord server](/guides/official-discord-server/).
2. Complete the [Discord Linked Roles](/guides/discord-linked-roles/) flow and claim the **Verified** role (**server name → Linked Roles**).
3. Open the Verified VTC role request channel.
4. Click **Request Role** on the panel message.
5. In the form:
   - Leave the manager field **empty** to grant the role only to yourself, or
   - Enter the **Discord user ID** of one VTC manager you want to nominate (numeric ID, not a display name).
6. Submit the form. You receive an ephemeral reply with the result.

If both slots are granted, you and your nominated manager receive the role within a few seconds.

### Nominating a manager later or changing manager

Submit a new request with a different manager Discord user ID. The system replaces the current manager slot if one is already filled. Your owner slot is updated on each request.

### Rate limit

You can submit one request per minute per Discord account.

## View status on TrucklineMP

VTC owners can see who holds the role from the website:

1. Open your VTC **Management Console**.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Scroll to **Main Discord Role**.

This section shows:

- Whether your VTC is verified
- Who holds the **owner** and **manager** slots (if assigned)
- A link to the request channel on Discord (when configured)

Only the VTC owner can view this section.

## When the role is removed automatically

The role is monitored. It is removed immediately when eligibility is lost. You do not keep the Discord role after your VTC or position no longer qualifies.

Common reasons for removal:

| Event | What happens |
|-------|----------------|
| VTC loses **verified** status | All grants for that VTC are revoked |
| VTC is **disbanded** | All grants for that VTC are revoked |
| **Ownership transfer** | The previous owner's grant is revoked. The new owner must request again |
| Member **removed** from the VTC | That member's grant is revoked |
| Manager **loses management permissions** | That manager's grant is revoked |
| **Discord unlinked** from TrucklineMP | All grants for that user are revoked |
| **Verified** Linked Role removed on Discord | Grant is revoked on the next eligibility check |

There is no grace period after verification is revoked. Plan to re-request only after your VTC is verified again and you still meet all requirements.

## Troubleshooting

### "You must own a verified VTC"

Your VTC does not have active Verified Program status, or you are not the owner. Check **Verified Status** in your management panel.

### "You need the Verified linked role"

Claim the **Verified** role through **server name → Linked Roles** on the main Discord. Connecting Discord under trucklinemp.com **Settings → Connections** alone does not satisfy this requirement.

### "The nominated user must have a VTC Management position"

The person you entered is not a VTC member with management permissions. Assign them the **Management** role (or equivalent permissions) in your VTC first.

### "The nominated manager must have the Verified linked role"

Your manager must complete the Linked Role opt-in on the main server before you nominate them.

### "This user already holds the role for another VTC"

Each person can only hold this role for one VTC at a time.

### "You own multiple verified VTCs"

The automated flow supports one verified VTC per owner. Contact Truckline staff if you need help for an edge case.

### Role missing after a successful request

- Confirm you are on the **main** TrucklineMP Discord server, not a staff or beta server.
- Wait a few seconds and check your roles again.
- If it still fails, open a [support ticket](/guides/support-tickets/) with your VTC name and Discord username.

## How this differs from other Discord features

| Feature | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| [Discord Linked Roles](/guides/discord-linked-roles/) | Opt-in **Verified** role for any member with a linked TrucklineMP account |
| [VTC Discord Verification](/guides/vtc-discord-verification/) | Link **your VTC's Discord server** to your TrucklineMP VTC profile |
| **Verified VTC Manager/Owner** (this guide) | Leadership role on the **main** TrucklineMP Discord for verified VTC owners and one manager |

You need the **Verified** Linked Role before you can request the Verified VTC Manager/Owner role.
