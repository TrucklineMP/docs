# Disbanding a VTC

Disbanding permanently removes your VTC from TrucklineMP. Only the **VTC owner** can disband. This action cannot be undone.

## Before you disband

Consider alternatives:

- Transfer ownership to a trusted member ([Member Management](/guides/vtc-member-management/))
- Set visibility to **private** and close recruitment ([Visibility](/guides/vtc-visibility/))
- Pause operations but keep the VTC for a future return

Disbanding is appropriate when the company is shutting down for good.

---

## How to disband

1. Open your VTC **Manage** panel
2. Go to **General Settings** (`/vtc/@handle/manage/settings`)
3. Scroll to the danger zone
4. Choose **Disband VTC**
5. Confirm when prompted (you may need to type the VTC name)

Only the owner sees this option.

---

## What happens

When a VTC is disbanded:

- The VTC profile and manage panel are removed
- All members lose their membership in this VTC
- VTC news, events, and recruitment data tied to the VTC are removed or archived per platform policy
- Discord server verification for this VTC is revoked
- Active invite links stop working
- The handle may become available for reuse after a cooling period (not guaranteed)

Members are **not** deleted from TrucklineMP. They simply no longer belong to the disbanded VTC.

---

## Discord and announcements

If you linked a Discord server:

- `/vtc` and `/verify-server` will report the server as unlinked
- Announcement webhooks stop firing for this VTC
- Unlink manually in Discord if you want a clean break before disbanding

See [Discord Verification](/guides/vtc-discord-verification/).

---

## Staff disbandment

Platform staff can disband VTCs that violate rules. Staff actions are logged separately. If your VTC was removed by staff, contact [Support](/guides/support-tickets/) with questions.
